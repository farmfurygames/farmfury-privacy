Privacy Policy — Farm Fleet

Last Updated: April 18, 2026

1. Introduction

Farm Fleet ("the Game") is a free-to-play mobile game developed by Farm Fury Games ("we", "us", "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information.

Contact: farmfurygames@gmail.com

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Google AdMob

We use Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect:

  • Device advertising ID (GAID/IDFA)
  • IP address (for ad targeting and fraud prevention)
  • Device type, OS version
  • Ad interaction data (impressions, clicks)

You choose between personalized and non-personalized ads on first launch. You can change this in Settings > Ad Preferences at any time.

For personalized ads, Google may use your advertising ID to show relevant ads based on your interests. For non-personalized ads, Google uses contextual information only.

Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

2.2 Time Verification (World Time API)

On launch the game makes one request to worldtimeapi.org to verify your device clock (this prevents cheating the daily rewards by changing the clock). This request discloses your device's IP address to that service. No other data is sent and nothing is stored. If the service is unreachable the game plays normally with no penalty.

World Time API terms: https://worldtimeapi.org

3. How Information Is Used

  • AdMob: To display ads and generate revenue to support free gameplay
  • Time verification: To detect device-clock tampering of daily rewards (IP only, not stored)
  • We do NOT sell, share, or transfer your personal data to third parties beyond these services

4. Data Retention

  • AdMob data: Managed by Google per their retention policies
  • Game save data: Stored locally in the app's private storage on your device only; uninstalling the app deletes it

5. Children's Privacy

Farm Fleet is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The game contains no user accounts, social features, or personal data collection beyond ad delivery.

6. App Store Rating Prompt

The game may ask you to rate it on the Google Play Store after several play sessions. This prompt:

  • Does not collect any data
  • Does not share any information
  • Can be permanently dismissed by tapping "No Thanks"

7. Your Rights

  • You can reset your ad preferences at any time in Settings
  • You can opt out of personalized ads through your device's ad settings

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted here with an updated "Last Updated" date.
